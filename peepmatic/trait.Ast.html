Expand description

A trait implemented by all AST nodes.

All AST nodes can:

  • Enumerate their children via ChildNodes.

  • Give you the wast::Span where they were defined.

  • Be converted into a DynAstRef.

This trait is blanked implemented for everything that does those three things, and in practice those three thrings are all implemented by the derive(Ast) macro.
